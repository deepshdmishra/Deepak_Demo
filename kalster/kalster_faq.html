What kind of hardware and computers do I need
All you need to get started is computer with internet.
2.
What is the limit on the number of patients or users?
There are no limits on users, records or patients in your account. Our system automatically grows as your practice grows.
3.
What are the benefits of cloud based software?
Our cloud-based infrastructure means that one secure login gives you access to your data from everywhere, anytime. Our high-availability Service Level Agreement ensures that your account is always accessible. We take care of hardware, software and security for your data.

Yes, absolutely! All your patient data is encrypted and no one (even our team) can read it unless it's authorized from your side. We sign an exculsive Data Security agreement with Product License agreement that gurantees the same.
